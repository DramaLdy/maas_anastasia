    Auditions

    To help you prepare for auditions, we have compiled some audition songs that might be appropriate per voice type. Please ask if you have questions or concerns. We are so excited to have you as a part of this show!




    Auditioning for Anya/Princess Anastasia
    Anya is a scrappy and determined young woman with no memory of her former life as a Romanov Princess after escaping the Bolshevik uprising as a child. She begins a journey of self-discovery on her way to Paris with two con artists to be reunited with the family she is certain is waiting for her– and falls in love on the way.

    Auditioning for Dmitry
    Dmitry is a conman with great comedic timing and charm despite his lower status. His scheme to present Anya as the long lost Princess Anastasia for a reward goes awry when he falls in love, transforms his moral compass, and gives a great sacrifice.

    Auditioning for Dowager Empress Maria Feodorovna
    The Dowager Empress fled to Paris where years later she is still searching for her last surviving relative, her granddaughter Princess Anastasia. A former royal, she has become cynical from years of turning away actresses and fortune seekers. When Anya meets the Empress and finally breaks her exterior, she finds the everlasting warmth and love of family she has been missing.

    Auditioning for Countess Lily Malevsky-Malevitch
    Countess Lily is the Dowager Empress’ right-hand in Paris, and the jovial and passionate comedic counterpart to Vlad Popov. She orchestrates the Empress’ search for the real Princess Anya while being endlessly energetic and nostalgic for her former life as a Russian noble. She is delighted to be reunited with Vlad who has accompanied Anya and Dmitry to Paris.

    Auditioning for Vlad Popov
    Russian ex-noble Vlad schemes with conman Dmitry to present Anya as the lost Anastasia. Although he initially faked his way into nobility and now seeks the Empress’ reward and a reunion with his former lover Countess Lily, he ultimately drops all personal gains to reunite Anya with her family. He is witty, genuinely good-hearted, and the comedic counterpart to Countess Lily.

    Auditioning for Gleb Vaganov
    Gleb is an ambitious young Bolshevik officer who dutifully enforces the party’s continued reign in St. Petersburg. His mission is to annihilate the rumors of Anastasia’s survival, but he is conflicted by his affection for her.

    Auditioning for Tsarina Alexandra
    Regal, yet wrought with uncertainty and dread for the alleged uprising, Tsarina Alexandra is a stern but loving mother.
